Operational History

1985

Foundation & Initial Deployment

TITAN INDUSTRIAL is established to provide high-grade structural steel frameworks for domestic manufacturing facilities. First continuous production line activated.

1998

Automation Protocol Integration

Implementation of automated robotic welding systems across all primary facilities, increasing production output precision by 400% while standardizing safety baseline.

2012

Global Logistics Expansion

Launched maritime heavy-lift division and opened high-capacity specialized ports in Europe and Asia, solidifying TITAN's international structural supply chain.
