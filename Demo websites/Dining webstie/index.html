Heritage &
Craftsmanship

Founded by Chef Elena Rostova, Lumina Bistro was born from a desire to strip away the pretense of fine dining while maintaining its rigorous standards.

Our philosophy is simple: source the absolute best ingredients locally, apply classical techniques with a modern sensibility, and serve it in a space that feels like an extension of our home. We believe that true luxury is found in authenticity and the quiet details.
